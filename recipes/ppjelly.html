Peanut Butter Jelly Time!

Description

Such a simple sandwich!

Ingredients Needed

  • Jif Creamy or Crunchy - Skippy...etc (Whatever brand you prefer)
  • Smucker's grape or strawberry...etc (Whatever brand you prefer...)
  • Wheat or White Bread (Any brand of your choosing, honestly)

    • Toaster is optional

How-to Basic

    Optional: Toast your bread first, allowing you time for some preparation utensils.

  1. Have your spoon ready to start taking one or two tablespoons of peanut butter and spread it on side of the bread.
  2. Now, have your spoon ready to start taking one or two tablespoons of jelly and spread it on the side without peanut butter.
  3. Lastly, combined both breads together for some magic!
